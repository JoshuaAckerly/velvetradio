# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Graveyard Jokes Studios ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Velvet Radio (velvetradio.graveyardjokes.com).

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit this site, we automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate geographic location
- Browser type and version
- Visit timestamps and page views

When you register an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Username and password (hashed)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the service
- Manage user accounts (show and venue management)
- Understand site usage and improve performance
- Ensure security

## 3. Third-Party Services

- **Zoho Mail** — for account-related notifications

We do not sell your personal information.

## 4. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for authentication and CSRF protection. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for details.

## 5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.

## 6. Your Rights

To access, correct, or request deletion of your data, contact us at [admin@graveyardjokes.com](mailto:admin@graveyardjokes.com).

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted with an updated effective date.
