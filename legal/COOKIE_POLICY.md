# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Velvet Radio (velvetradio.graveyardjokes.com) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit this site, operated by Graveyard Jokes Studios.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly and may be used to understand how the site is used.

## 2. Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

We use a single essential session cookie (laravel_session) required for basic site functionality:

- **Session Cookie** (laravel_session) — Required for CSRF protection and site security. This cookie:
  - Is valid for the entire site (Path: `/`)
  - Is HTTPOnly (not accessible via JavaScript)
  - Uses SameSite: `lax` for CSRF protection
  - Is sent over HTTPS in production

### No Analytics Cookies

We do not use third-party analytics cookies on this site. Only the essential session cookie described above is set.

## 3. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling the session cookie may prevent the site from functioning correctly.

## 4. Consent

By continuing to use this site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
